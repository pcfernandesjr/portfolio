About Me


Hi, I'm Paulo Fernandes - A brazilian Designer focused in User Experience and Interface Design.

My career started in 2009, when I got a job offer to work for a mobile app development startup after working for a couple of months in a printing bureau.

From that point onwards I had the opportunity to work on several cool projects such as a World Cup application for a Brazilian Sports Magazine, a HelpDesk software, a SD-WAN Configuration portal, and a Conferencing service for a UK based Telecom Company, Fingerprint, a Facial Capture software used by the Brazilian Federal Police, a demo portal for a big Virtualization and Cloud Solutions provider based on USA, among others.
